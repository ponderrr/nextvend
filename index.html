From Roommates to Business Partners

Spencer and Finn, both from Mandeville, Louisiana, moved to Houston to pursue their sales careers - Spencer in medical sales and Finn as an account executive. But they never forgot their shared dream of entrepreneurship that started back home.

As roommates in Houston, they combined their sales expertise and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction to launch NextVend. Their proven track record in building client relationships and delivering results gives them the edge to serve businesses across Texas and Louisiana.

With customer satisfaction as their top priority, Spencer and Finn are leveraging their sales mastery to accommodate businesses of all sizes. Their vision: expand NextVend's footprint across their home state and throughout Texas, one satisfied customer at a time.
