Our Journey

250+ Machines Deployed
2 States Served

From Local to Regional

Founded in 2018 in New Orleans, NextStop Vending Co. started with just 5 machines in local businesses. Our commitment to quality service and cutting-edge technology quickly set us apart from traditional vending operators.

Today, we manage over 250 machines across Louisiana and Texas, serving diverse locations from corporate offices to university campuses. Our growth continues as we expand our territory and enhance our service offerings.

We've built our reputation on reliability, innovation, and exceptional customer service. As we grow, these values remain at the core of everything we do.
